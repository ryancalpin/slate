Which standard modules should ship out of the box?

Select all you'd want in the default library. Users can always build custom modules too — this is just what comes pre-built.

01

Patient Header

Room, age, sex, admit date, attending, service, diagnosis, code status, isolation precautions

02

Vitals

HR, BP, RR, Temp, SpO2, Weight. Trend arrows, configurable normal ranges, color-coded alerts

03

Labs

BMP, CBC, LFTs, coags, custom panels. Prior-value comparison with trend indicators

04

Assessment & Plan

Problem-based SOAP. Numbered problem list, each with sub-fields for assessment and plan items

05

Medications

Active med list with dose/route/frequency. Highlight key meds (pressors, anticoagulants, abx)

06

Intake & Output

24h I/O, net balance, UOP (mL/hr), fluid boluses, drip rates

07

Lines / Tubes / Drains

PIV, CVC, arterial line, Foley, NGT, chest tube — with insertion date/site

08

Task Checklist

Checkbox list of pending tasks/orders. Mark complete, add notes, assign to role

09

Free Text / Notes

Rich text or plain text area for HPI, overnight events, impressions, anything narrative

10

Consults & Results

Track active consults (service, question, status/recs) and pending imaging/procedure results

11

Nursing Assessments

Head-to-toe system review: neuro, cardiac, resp, GI, GU, skin, mobility, fall risk, pain/CPOT

12

Custom Field Module

User-defined fields: text, number, checkbox, dropdown, date, or calculated value — fully configurable
